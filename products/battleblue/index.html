BattleBlue

The BattleBlue is a tiny, lightweight, all-in-one solution for combat robotics. Remotely control up to 2 brushed motors and output 2 PWM signals for brushless motor ESCs using an app for your Android smartphone (iOS support coming soon).
Around the world, the sport of Combat Robotics is popular and growing. This is particularly true for the 150-gram, 1-pound, and 3-pound weight classes, with over 6500 fights in the last two years. Despite the popularity, the controls system used to wirelessly control robots was designed decades ago for RC aircraft, and today is seriously outdated and expensive. The Battleblue is a lightweight and inexpensive Bluetooth-based robot control system.

Key features:
  • Small
  • Lightweight
  • Controls 2 brushed DC motors, perfect for a drivetrain
  • 2 configurable PWM channels, perfect for brushless ESCs or servos
  • Controlled using a smartphone app instead of an expensive transmitter

    • App features:
  • Runs on any device with Android 7.0 and up
  • Configurable on-screen joysticks, buttons, and sliders
  • Built-in channel mixing
  • Advanced channel mixing
  • iOS and physical controller support coming soon
